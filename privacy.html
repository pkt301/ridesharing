Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Rido ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ridesharing application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and password
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, driver's license information for verification purposes
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and payment history
  • Profile Information: Preferences, vehicle information (for drivers), and ratings

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location, trip routes, and destination information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction data
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, and pages viewed

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our ridesharing services
  • Match drivers and passengers for rides
  • Process payments and send receipts
  • Verify user identity and ensure platform safety
  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Basic profile information (name, photo, rating) is shared with matched drivers/passengers
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (payment processing, cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Rido, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition of assets

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Secure payment processing

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Location Services: Control location sharing through device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us at pktiwari110487@gmail.com.

7. Location Data

Location data is essential for our ridesharing services. We collect location information:

  • When you use the app to request or offer a ride
  • During active rides for real-time tracking and safety
  • To provide accurate pickup and drop-off locations

You can control location sharing through your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze app performance and usage
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements

You can control cookie preferences through your browser or device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
